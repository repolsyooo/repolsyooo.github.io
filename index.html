Hello! I’m Nataniel Harold S. Abagat, a dedicated 1st-year Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) student at the Camarines Sur Polytechnic Colleges (CSPC)—proudly studying at the premier institution of Camarines Sur.

As an aspiring developer, I find my rhythm in the world of code. I’m currently focused on mastering HTML and CSS, transforming abstract ideas into functional, high-contrast designs. I am also diving deeper into JavaScript to bring interactivity and life to my projects. My goal isn't just to write code, but to architect large-scale digital solutions that bridge the gap between imagination and reality. I also love the synergy of using AI as my assistant to transform my ideas into functional designs using HTML and CSS. For me, coding is about the vision and the flow, and I’m constantly learning how to use modern tools to sharpen my ability to build big projects.

Beyond the screen and the syntax, my life is fueled by a love for storytelling and family. I grew up in a lively household with three siblings—two brothers and one sister. They are my constant support system and the reason I strive to be better every day.

When I’m not debugging or designing, I’m a massive consumer of visual media. Whether it’s the high-stakes action of Anime, the emotional depth of KDramas, or the immersive worlds of Manga and Short Films, I’m always looking for creative inspiration. You might also find me lost in a book or testing my reflexes in Call of Duty: Mobile, Minecraft, or Genshin Impact. These hobbies aren't just distractions; they help me understand user experience and visual storytelling from a different perspective.
